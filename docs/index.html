Flushing style

Tomorrow is just a dream away.

Elegant, clean and indifferent style guide for documents, websites and software.

Main features

Elegance

Flushing style has as its main goal the generation of elegant and clean content that transmits feelings of order, purity, indifference and atemporality.

Visual experience

Most of the colour palette is dominant towards blue, which favours a sense of coldness and disconnection from the natural world.

Deep roots

Flushing is inspired by the neo-futurist style of architects like Norman Foster or Santiago Calatrava.

With its head in the clouds

It happens to be a minimalistic visual style, somewhat disconnected from the imperfections of reality in order to form its own utopic isolated space.

Information first

Flushing doesn’t strive for closeness or integration, but simply to transmit information in an efficient and neutral way.

Multipurpose

Flushing is designed to work with text documents, presentations, websites and software apps, allowing for a unified experience no matter the form factor.
